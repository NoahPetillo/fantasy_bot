How to find your two cookies (60 seconds)
  1. Open ESPN Fantasy in a new tab and make sure you're logged in.
  2. Open your browser's developer tools:
    Chrome / Edge: press F12 (Mac: ⌥⌘I) → Application tab → Cookies → https://www.espn.com
    Safari: Settings → Advanced → "Show features for web developers", then Develop → Show Web Inspector → Storage → Cookies
    Firefox: press F12 → Storage tab → Cookies
  3. Find the cookie named espn_s2 and copy its whole value — it's long, around 200+ characters.
  4. Find the cookie named SWID and copy its value including the curly braces, like {1234ABCD-…}.
  5. Paste both below, tick the consent box, and hit Connect. Cookies stay valid for months unless you log out of ESPN.
